Caged Bird

Maya Angelou

Stanza Summary
1 A free bird enjoying his freedom and feels so proud to dare to claim the sky. He spents his time enjoying the priviliges he got.
2 A shift to a caged bird who is suffering from the lack of the freeom the first bird got. He is angry and although his chances to be free are low and his wings are clipped and his feet are tied hes still hopeful and starts to sing
3 The caged bird is singing with fear but is still hopeful for what lies beyond the cage. More than anything the bird sings and hopes for freedom.
4 All the free bird can think about is his continueous priviliges and it seems like he isn't aware that there are some birds that their fate is so much different and chooses tp turn a blind eye to it.
5 Nearly all hope is gone from the caged bird. he is full of sarrow and pain and yet he still hopes and opens his throat to sing.
6 Repeating Stanza 3
Theres a lot of contrast in the song. the free bird experiencing joy and free space, he has a lot of self esteem and confindence. Nothing steps on his way and he feels free to do and think how ever he likes.
On the oppisite side, the caged bird experiencing extreme boundry and inability to have the things that the free bird is already used to. All he can do is sing for a better future for himself.
The dream of the poet is for every bird (meaning every human) to have the possibility for a limitless future, to stop caging birds and stopping them fro expressing themselves and contribute to nature (the world/humanity)
